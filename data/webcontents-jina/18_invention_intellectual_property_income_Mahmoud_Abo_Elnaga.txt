### Steps in Details:

#### Step(1): Developing a plan for dissemination the final project

*   The project is not a new idea, it is an issue at most fab labs and makerspaces and each one contributes some solutions from his side.
*   The project will be an open-source and non-commercial.
*   The project is still a prototype and need further improvements and features to make it a final product. for example, it needs to be modular in the electronics side, the application needs to be more user friendly, it should have a smart database, and more and more features.
*   After Fab Academy, I will continue working on it, taking feedback form the lab members to improve its functions and usage.

*   For the **License**, I prefered to choose the Creative Common License and the MIT license:

*   **Creative Common License:** It is a free standard license which provides others a permission to use your work as per your condition. It helps us to share knowledge and creativity in a legal way. This work is licensed under a [Creative Commons Attribution-NonCommercial 4.0 International License](http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-nc/4.0/).
*   **MIT License:** MIT license permits reuse within proprietary software provided that all copies of the licensed software include a copy of the MIT License terms and the copyright notice. I prefer this license in future if I worked on softwares.
