              <h2>The "Doing"</h2>
              <h3  id="thedoing1">What have I done?</h3>
              <section class="section_assign">
                At some parts, it is quite difficult to make clear divisions of the work, because we have participated in smaller or bigger subgroups in almost all of the steps of the design. <br/>
                I was with Megumi trying to find an idea for a second group and thinking about the mechanism of a Lathe machine, I started to see a peeling arm and a fruit as the 3d shape. Then we asked Eino about it, and he thought it was good enough for the assignment. And then Behnaz, Heidi and Peetu liked the idea and joined us. 
                Here is the summary of what I have participated in for the machining design:
                <ul>
                  <li>I came up with the idea when talking with Megumi: From Lathe machine to apple peeler</li>

                  <li>I participated in the design of the structure: taking into account which parts we had from previous year's projects</li>

 <a href="img/assignment15/IMG_20180503_133729.jpg" title="" data-lightbox="portfolio" data-title="design"> <img src="img/assignment15/IMG_20180503_133729.jpg" alt="design" class="img-responsive img-border"></a><div class="caption"></div>

                  <li>I chose the parts needed from previous project to define an structure</li>
                   <a href="img/assignment15/IMG_20180503_133723.jpg" title="" data-lightbox="portfolio" data-title="design"> <img src="img/assignment15/IMG_20180503_133723.jpg" alt="design" class="img-responsive img-border"></a><div class="caption"></div>
                  <li>I participated in the first assembling of the pieces: I put together the base of the L structure so they would move as one back and forwards</li>

                  <li>I participated in the design of the piece to the rotating structure: I measure the parts to be joint together </li>
                  <a href="img/assignment15/Picture4.jpg" title="" data-lightbox="portfolio" data-title="design"> <img src="img/assignment15/Picture4.jpg" alt="design" class="img-responsive img-border"></a><div class="caption"></div>
                  <li>I started building the peeling mechanism (Use the metallic fold and how) 
                    <a href="img/assignment15/IMG_20180509_124302.jpg" title="" data-lightbox="portfolio" data-title="design"> <img src="img/assignment15/IMG_20180509_124302.jpg" alt="design" class="img-responsive img-border"></a><div class="caption"></div>
                    <ul><li> First, I cut a piece of the fold to test if just by hand I could peel the apple</li>
                    <li>I did the first prototype, attaching with tape to an MDF plate the blade and simulate the mechanism to see if it would work and how could it be attached to the structure</li>
                    <li>I came with the idea of how to attach the blade to the MDF and, together with Megumi, got the measurements to design and then cut the MDF plate</li></ul></li>

                    <div class="row">
                      <div class="col-md-6">

                     </div> 
                     </div> 
                  <li>I have done most of the layout, organization and documentation in the group page</li>
                </ul>

                There are still many things missing and many parts that wold need some revisiting during the next week.
